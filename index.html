Our Story

1KE was born from the Shanghai Web Dev and Design meetup group. After talking with hundreds of both local Chinese and International Expats, we realized that startups around the globe shared two things: a desire to share knowledge and difficulty attracting users in early stages. Headquartered out of Shanghai and San Francisco, we created 1KE to bring the international tech community together.
